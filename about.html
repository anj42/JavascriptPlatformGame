About the game:

"Like the Wind" is a fast-paced platform jumper.

All graphics except for the game background were drawn by Antoni Janas. Credit list for the author and sound artists is situated at the bottom of this page.

Controls:

Player character can be controlled by either mouse or key clicks.

    Mouse controls: hold down the left button in order to keep running. Release the button to jump.

    Keyboard controls: hold down the right arrow in order to keep running. Press the up arrow to jump.
